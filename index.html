Maritime Finance & ESG

A carbon cost optimization platform for shipping that helps fleets manage EU ETS exposure, allocate responsibility across stakeholders, and execute carbon purchasing more efficiently.

1 Problem

Shipping companies face a new financial burden under EU ETS. Today, carbon exposure is often managed through spreadsheets, fragmented operational data, and manual coordination between shipowners, charterers, and brokers.

2 Solution

We provide a platform that turns emissions-related compliance into a controllable financial workflow. It tracks vessel-level exposure, supports carbon purchase decisions, and makes role allocation between owner and charterer clearer and easier to execute.

3 Why now

EU shipping compliance is already becoming operationally and financially material. The market needs software that sits between operations, finance, and carbon execution - not another spreadsheet workflow.

Business model

Subscription per vessel, plus transaction-based revenue on carbon-related execution and optional premium automation modules.

Status

Architecture defined, MVP in development, stakeholder discussions initiated, and external data integrations planned.

Vision

Become the operating layer for carbon compliance and carbon-linked financial workflows in shipping. We build a platform as service for shipowner to join our cloud or on premises

Who it serves

Primary users: Shipowners, charterers, ship managers, maritime finance teams.

Core value: Better cost control, clearer accountability, and more structured carbon purchasing.
